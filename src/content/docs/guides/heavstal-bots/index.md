Welcome to the **Heavstal Bots Platform** guide. 

[Heavstal Bots](https://bots.heavstal.com.ng) is your central command center for interacting with Heavstal Tech's third-party automation agents like our flagship WhatsApp bot, **Verselor-V1**.

## Platform Overview

Rather than interacting with complex code or confusing terminal commands, the Heavstal Bots platform gives you a clean, user-friendly dashboard to manage your bot experience. 

Its primary focus is **Authentication and Access Management**. Through this platform, you can:

- **Generate AuthCodes:** Create secure, unique keys to link your personal accounts to our bots.
- **Manage Access:** Grant or revoke bot permissions seamlessly.
- **Unlock Premium Features:** Manage your subscriptions and apply premium tags to your account for advanced bot capabilities.
